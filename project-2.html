Geospatial Analytics App

A geospatial analysis app that visualizes the distribution and adequacy of train stations across Indonesia. I developed the thematic mapping engine, polygon rendering pipeline, and multithreaded GeoJSON preprocessing to ensure smooth, accurate, and interactive exploration of regional infrastructure data.

Repository

Project Overview

The application provides an interactive geospatial visualization of train stations across Indonesia and the adequacy of their distribution relative to provincial population data. I built the thematic mapping engine that renders province-level polygons, assigns colors based on population-to-station ratios, and clusters station markers for efficient exploration.

To ensure smooth performance while processing large GeoJSON datasets, I implemented background isolates (multithreading) for polygon parsing and data preprocessing.

This module serves as the analytical core of the system, enabling users to quickly interpret regional disparities, identify underserved areas, and explore detailed station information directly through the map interface.
